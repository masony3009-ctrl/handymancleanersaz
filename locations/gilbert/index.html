The Turnover

What Your $200 Gilbert Cleaning Includes

The flat rate covers a complete turnover for up to five beds. We strip, wash, dry, and remake the property's linens on-site; clean and sanitize the kitchen and bathrooms; dust and reset living spaces; vacuum and mop floors; empty trash; and stage the home for the next arrival.

We also check the property against your checklist, note visible damage or maintenance concerns, and send photo or video walkthrough proof before we lock up. Guest consumables can be restocked from supplies you ship to us or from a funded restock run.

Gilbert Airbnb Cleaning FAQ

Questions From Local Hosts

How much is Airbnb turnover cleaning in Gilbert?

Our standard turnover is $200 flat for properties with up to five beds. It includes on-site linen laundry and the full guest-ready reset. Homes with more than five beds receive a custom quote.

Can you handle a same-day Gilbert turnover?

Yes. Same-day turnovers are the same price when we have the checkout and check-in window. Request by 9 AM whenever possible so we can route the property correctly.

Do you restock guest supplies?

Yes. You can ship paper goods, soaps, coffee, and other guest supplies to us, or fund us to purchase approved items. We report low inventory before it becomes a guest problem.

Can you fix small maintenance problems between guests?

Yes. Our handyman rate is $100 for the first hour and $80 for each additional hour. We can handle many punch-list items during the same property visit.
