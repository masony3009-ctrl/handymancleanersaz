Standard checklist

What we reset during a basic turnover.

Hosts can add their own checklist or request extra cleaning items in the form.

Arrival and walkthrough

  • Arrive on time
  • Report damage or missing items
  • Remove trash and replace liners
  • Final walkthrough before departure

Bedrooms and living areas

  • Strip and replace bedding
  • Make beds hotel-style
  • Dust, vacuum, mop, and sanitize high-touch areas
  • Arrange furniture neatly

Kitchen and bathrooms

  • Wash and put away dishes
  • Clean counters, cabinets, microwave, fridge, and appliance exteriors
  • Scrub toilets, sinks, tubs, and showers
  • Clean mirrors, replace towels, refill toiletries, and mop floors

Laundry and outdoor reset

  • Wipe washer and dryer
  • Organize linens
  • Blow out patios
  • Clean and arrange outdoor furniture and grill grates
