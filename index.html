Our Standard

The Turnover Checklist

Every turnover follows a four-part process to ensure your property is guest-ready.

Arrival and Walkthrough

  • Arrive on time
  • Report damage and missing items
  • Remove trash and replace bin liners
  • Final walkthrough with proof before departure

Bedrooms and Living Areas

  • Strip and replace all bedding
  • Make beds to hotel standards
  • Dust, vacuum, mop, and sanitize high-touch surfaces
  • Arrange furniture neatly

Kitchen and Bathrooms

  • Wash and put away all dishes
  • Clean counters, cabinets, microwave, refrigerator, and appliance exteriors
  • Scrub toilets, sinks, tubs, and showers
  • Clean mirrors, restock towels and toiletries, mop floors

Laundry and Outdoor Reset

  • Wipe down washer and dryer
  • Organize and store all linens
  • Blow out patios
  • Clean and arrange outdoor furniture and grill grates

FAQ

Host Questions, Answered

What's included in the $200 flat rate?

The $200 covers a complete turnover for up to 5 beds, including stripping, washing, and remaking linens on-site. Each additional bed beyond 5 is $50 — a 7-bed property, for example, is $300.

Can I schedule a same-day turnover?

Yes, at the same flat rate with no rush fee. Request by 9am that morning and we'll add it to the day's schedule.

Do you provide cleaning supplies, or do I?

We bring all of our own cleaning equipment and supplies. Guest consumables like paper goods, soaps, and coffee are separate — you either ship them to us or fund restock runs.

How do you handle washing and remaking the beds?

We strip all beds and wash the linens on-site, then remake them immediately. We recommend keeping a second set of linens per bed so rooms are ready while laundry runs.

Can I use my own cleaning checklist?

Yes. Send or upload your checklist with your turnover request and we'll follow it. Otherwise, we use our standard checklist.

What happens if you find damage during the turnover?

You get a text with photos and a full explanation immediately when we find it. Every turnover also ends with walkthrough proof — an exit video or photo notes — so you have clear documentation of the property's condition.

What payment methods do you accept, and when is payment due?

We accept Zelle, Venmo, Cash App, PayPal, Turno, or invoice. Payment is due after each cleaning is complete.

What's your cancellation policy, and do you work weekends and holidays?

Cancellation is free up to 24 hours before the scheduled start; canceling within the final 24 hours is a $75 fee. We work all hours, weekends, and holidays — holiday cleans add $50.
