The Service

Cleaned After Close, Ready by Open

We clean small-business offices after hours — in the evening or overnight, once your team has gone home. Nobody works around a vacuum, no wet floors during business hours, and no disruption to customers or staff. You lock up, we clean, and the office is fresh before the first person arrives the next morning.

It's the same father-and-son team, the same reliability, and the same walkthrough-proof standard our vacation-rental hosts count on — applied to your workspace on a recurring schedule that fits how often you need it.

What We Clean

Every Recurring Office Visit

  • Restrooms cleaned and sanitized
  • Breakrooms and kitchenettes wiped down
  • Desks and workstations dusted
  • Reception and entry areas reset
  • Common spaces and conference rooms
  • Trash emptied and liners replaced
  • Carpets and rugs vacuumed
  • Hard floors swept and mopped
  • High-touch surfaces disinfected
  • Supply restocking on request
