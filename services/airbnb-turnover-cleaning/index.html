FAQ

Airbnb Host Questions

Can you sync with my Airbnb calendar?

Yes. Share your Airbnb calendar link once and every checkout becomes a scheduled turnover automatically — new bookings, changed dates, and back-to-back stays included. You never have to message us a booking again. See how calendar sync works →

Can you handle same-day Airbnb turnovers?

Yes, at the same $200 flat rate with no rush fee. Request by 9am that morning and we handle the checkout-to-check-in window — it's the core of what we do.

What happens if a guest damages something?

The moment we find damage you get a text with photos and a full explanation. Every turnover also ends with a walkthrough video, so you have time-stamped documentation of the property's condition to support a damage claim if you ever need to file one.
