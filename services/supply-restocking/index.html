The Service

Stocked Every Visit, Without You Thinking About It

An empty toilet-paper roll or a bare coffee station is a small thing that leaves a big impression — a bad review from a guest, or a grumble from your team on Monday morning. Because we're already on-site for your turnover or office clean, we fold restocking right into the visit.

It works for both vacation rentals and offices, and there's no markup game: you either ship supplies to us to keep on hand, or fund a restock run and we buy what's needed.

How It Works

Two Simple Ways to Stay Stocked

  • Ship supplies to us — we keep them on hand and restock each visit
  • Fund a restock run — we buy what's needed and bring it along
  • Give us a simple par list of what to keep topped up
  • We flag anything running low so you can reorder in time
  • Works on turnovers, recurring cleans, and office visits
