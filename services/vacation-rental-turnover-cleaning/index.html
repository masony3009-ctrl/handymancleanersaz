The Service

More Than a Clean — a Full Guest-Ready Reset

A turnover isn't a house clean — it's a full guest-ready reset inside a tight checkout-to-check-in window, and it's all we do, every day. Every visit runs the same checklist and ends with walkthrough proof — an exit video or photo notes — sent straight to you.

Our Standard

The Turnover Checklist

General

  • Arrive on time
  • Report damage or missing items
  • Remove all trash
  • Restock provided supplies
  • Final walkthrough before departure

Living Areas

  • Dust all surfaces
  • Vacuum and mop floors
  • Sanitize high-touch areas
  • Arrange furniture neatly

Kitchen

  • Wash and put away dishes
  • Clean countertops and cabinets
  • Clean appliance exteriors
  • Clean inside microwave and fridge
  • Empty trash and mop floor

Bedrooms

  • Strip and replace bedding
  • Dust furniture and headboards
  • Vacuum floors and under beds
  • Sanitize switches and handles
  • Make beds hotel-style

Bathrooms

  • Scrub toilets, sinks, tubs, and showers
  • Clean mirrors and chrome
  • Replace towels
  • Refill toiletries
  • Mop floors and empty trash

Finishing Touches

  • Wipe washer and dryer
  • Organize linens
  • Blow out patios
  • Clean and arrange outdoor furniture
  • Set thermostat, check lights, send exit video, and secure property
Have your own checklist?

Send or upload it with your cleaning request and we'll follow it exactly — otherwise we run our standard process above.

FAQ

Turnover Questions, Answered

Do you provide cleaning supplies, or do I?

We bring all of our own cleaning equipment and supplies, or use yours if you prefer. Guest consumables like paper goods, soaps, and coffee are separate — you either ship them to us or fund restock runs.

How do you handle washing and remaking the beds?

We strip all beds and wash the linens on-site, then remake them immediately. We recommend keeping a second set of linens per bed so rooms are ready while laundry runs.

What happens if you find damage during the turnover?

The moment we find it, you get a text with photos and a full explanation.
