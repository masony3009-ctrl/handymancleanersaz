The Service

The Small Stuff, Handled Between Cleans

Rentals always have a running list of little things — too small to call a contractor out for, but they still need doing before the next guest. Because we're already on-site cleaning, we can knock those out in the same visit.

We focus on light, minor fixes and general upkeep. For anything that needs a licensed trade — major electrical, plumbing, or structural work — we'll tell you honestly and point you in the right direction rather than take it on.

Examples

Typical Light Fixes & Upkeep

  • Hanging shelves, art, mirrors, and hooks
  • Replacing light bulbs and batteries
  • Swapping air filters
  • Tightening handles, hinges, and fixtures
  • Assembling furniture and decor
  • Touch-ups and small adjustments
  • Replacing remotes, smoke-alarm batteries, and small hardware
  • General between-guest upkeep and resets

Not sure if it fits? Ask — we'll tell you straight whether it's a light fix or a job for a licensed trade.
